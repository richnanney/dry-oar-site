Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide synergistic solutions to your rafting needs. Through differentiating value-added strategy and transformational change, we aim to coordinate with customers to deliver real-time, professional rafting and oar-related experiences 24/7. Using state of the art modern applications, we're able to utilize collaboration and idea-sharing to effectively fulfill our promises. The goal of the website is to create cloud based, client focused content to address various action items relating to customer needs, as well as be able to extend an outreach of cross-platform inclusivity to those who wish touse our product. As the website develops, we hope that we can expedite the process to ensure this alternative resource maximizing opportunity is able to leverage both profit and satisfaction.

Audience

The target audience for our product is for those who are interested in white water rafting, mostly. Often they are young adults, but we see ages vary from the very young to the very old. If they're visiting the website, it is likely that they are seeking some information about our product, as well as ways to potentially get in contact with us. To ensure that their needs are met, we must be clear and concise regarding the services we provide. Because of the wide variety of ages, people will be accessing our website from various devices, such as laptops, desktops, and mobile devices.

Typography

Heading Font: Secular One

Seems like a nice, bold font that is slightly serifed. A strong font is eye-catching and good for heading fonts.

Paragraph Font: Helvetica

Helvetica is a mild yet clean font that is used all over the internet, most notably Twitter. A sans-serif font, Helvetica is very easy on the eyes and clean to read, making it suitable for pargraphs.

Normal paragraph example

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, White Water Rafting Company offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.

Trips vary from mild and great for families, to trips exclusively for physically fit and experienced rafters. No matter what type of river adventures you are seeking, White Water Rafting Company can make it happen for you.

The Site Map of a site is just like it sounds…it is a map of the pages in a site and how they are related and linked together. From the map above we can see that we will eventually have the Home page and 2 sub or child pages.

The lines that connect them all together indicate that each page should be accessible from any other page, it is essentially showing us the global navigation for the site.

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo (the box with the mountain means an image) and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
